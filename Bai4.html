5. Groups of selectors

A comma-separated list of selectors represent the union of the individual selectors in the list. (A comma is U+002C.) For example, in CSS when several selectors share the same declarations, they may be grouped into a comma-separated list. White space may appear before and/or after the camma.

h1, h2, h3 { font-family: sans-serif }
